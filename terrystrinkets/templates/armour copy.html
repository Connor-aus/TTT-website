ARMOURER

Light Armour

Padded Armour

Padded armour consists of quilted layers of cloth and batting.

Leather Armour

Some of this armour is made of leather that has been stiffened by being boiled in oil. The rest is made of softer and more flexible materials.

Studded Leather Armour

Made from tough but flexible leather, studded leather is reinforced with close-set rivets or spikes.

Medium Armour

Hide Armour

This crude armour consists of thick furs and pelts.

Chain Shirt

Made of interlocking metal rings, a chain shirt is worn between layers of clothing or leather. This armour offers modest protection to the wearer’s upper body.

Scale Armour

This armour consists of a coat and leggings (and perhaps a separate skirt) of leather covered with overlapping pieces of metal, much like the scales of a fish.

Breastplate Armour

This armour consists of a fitted metal chest piece worn with supple leather. Although it leaves the legs and arms relatively unprotected, this armour provides good protection for the wearer’s vital organs.

Half Plate Armour

Half plate consists of shaped metal plates that cover most of the wearer’s body. It does not include leg protection beyond simple greaves that are attached with leather straps.
